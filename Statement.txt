There is a very strange tradition in the family of Kapoors. Every man in the family has exactly 2 sons. 
Shubhash kapoor is the member of kapoor's family he is curious to know about the no of member in his family.Shubhash knew his genertion. You have to tell the number of members in his family upto his own generation.
The first generation is generation 0 which has only one member.

Input

Input starts with an integer T <= 1000000.

Each case contains an integer n. n<=63.

Output

For each test case, print no of members in his family.

Sample Input
3
1
3
0
Output for Sample Input
3
15
1
